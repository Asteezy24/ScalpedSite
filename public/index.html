Specify how low you want to buy a stock. When the stock dips, you buy. If the stock dips more, you buy more. you do not sell until your specified sell target. Organic portfolio growth with as determined by the team at school. Courtesy of Mike Deleon.

Targeted purchases based on price history.

By using industry-standard moving exponential moving averages (EMA) based on price history, you buy on the cross of the greater EMA over the smaller EMA. Sell when the cross happens again in the opposite direction.
